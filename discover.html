Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh

Famous for its majestic monastery, snow-capped peaks, and stunning landscapes. A perfect blend of spirituality and adventure.

Fun Fact: Tawang Monastery is the second largest in the world!

Cherrapunjee, Meghalaya

Known as one of the wettest places on Earth, home to living root bridges and lush greenery everywhere you look.

Fun Fact: Locals use living tree roots to build natural bridges!

Loktak Lake, Manipur

The only floating lake in the world, dotted with circular phumdis (floating islands of vegetation).

Fun Fact: Loktak Lake hosts the rare Sangai deer!
